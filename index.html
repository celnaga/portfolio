About me

I'm a Master's student in Analytics & Management at London Business School, with a focus on consumer-facing industries — the brands, platforms, and experiences that shape how people live and buy.

Before LBS, I worked as a business analyst on digital performance platforms for luxury brands — where data met desire. I did my undergraduate in China, which gave me a firsthand lens into how Eastern and Western markets think differently about identity, aspiration, and commerce.

Outside of work, I run, do Pilates, and spend far too much time on graphic design and content creation — which probably explains why I care as much about how an analysis looks as what it says.
