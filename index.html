Bio

I'm a Master's student in Analytics & Management at London Business School, passionate about how data can sharpen strategy for consumer-facing businesses — the brands, platforms, and experiences that shape how people live and buy. I gravitate toward problems at the intersection of human behaviour and business performance, where the right insight can shift a decision meaningfully.

Background & Experience

Before LBS, I worked as a business analyst on a digital performance platform for luxury brands — translating campaign data into commercial recommendations for premium clients. I completed my undergraduate degree in China, giving me firsthand exposure to how Eastern markets think about aspiration, community, and commerce differently to their Western counterparts. That dual perspective shapes how I approach every analytical problem.
